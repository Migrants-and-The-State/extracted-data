August 31, 1950 2854-P-15070 Mr. Low Bark long 23 Beatty Street Pawtucket, Rho de Island Dear Sir: In connection with your registration under the Selective Service and Training Act of 1940, will you kindly furnish the following required information on this letter: Name under which you registered: Number of Local Board where you registered. If unknown . please state the exact home address you gave at time of your registration: Any other information you may have which will aid the draft officials in locating your record: Your prompt attention to this matter is suggested, since further action on your petition cannot be taken until this matter is cleared up. Please return this letter with the desired information. Very truly yours, W. M. CLARK officer in Charge
