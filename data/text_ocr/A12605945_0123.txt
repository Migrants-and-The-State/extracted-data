U.S. DEPARTMENT OF LABOR IMMIGRATION SERVICE OFFICE OF THE COMMISSIONER SAN FRANCISCO, CAL. June 7, 1917. 14436/6-20. Inspector in charge, II. S. Immigration Service, Los Angeles, Cal. Referring to your letter of the 28th ultimo in the matter of Wong Quen Imake, requesting the return of the transcript of the habeas corpus record of Wong Shoon Jung, the alleged father of Wong Quen Luck, you are navised that the case in question was submitted to the Circuit Court of Appeals on habeau corpus last month, and that the transcript referred to being an exhibit in that case it will be possible to have the transcript withdrew until the case is decided by the Court, which will no doubt be in the near future. On the withdrawal of the exhibits the seid transcript will be forwarded to you for delivery to Wong Shoon Jung. copy as signed EDWARD WHITE Mailed this day by m CDM/LM Commissioner
