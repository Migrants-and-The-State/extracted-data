Application for extension should be made between 30 and 60 days prior to the expiration date shown on this permit. The application must contain (a) the name of the applicant, and his address in the United States: (b) when where and by what means he departed from the United States: (c) port of landing and date of arrival abroad; (d) countries visited in the order visited; (e) reason for requesting extension and period for which desired; and (1) applicant's foreign address to which permit is to be returned.
