In re Lee Gim Ngow, Minor son of Lee Yip Sang, a Native Born citizen of the United States. State of California, City and County of SS. San Francisco. ) Lee Hen Theu, being first duly sworn, deposes and says: That affiant is an uncle of Lee Gim Ngow. That affiant knows Lee Yip Sang, who makes the foregoing affidavit and whose photograph is thereto attached. That said Lee Yip Sang was born in the United States and is a citizen thereof, and that it has been so found and determined by competent authority. That said Lee Gim Ngow, whose photograph is also attached to the foregoing affidavit of Lee Yip Sang, is a lawful son of said Lee Yip Sang and is a minor. That said Lee Gim Ngow is a citizen of the United States, under and by virtue of the laws thereof, his father being a native born citizen of the United States. That the said Lee Xgxxx Gim Ngow is now in China, but is about to come to the United States to take up his residence therein. That a photograph of affiant is hereunto annexed. Subscribed and sworn to before me this 28 day of October, 1913. Notary Public in and for the City and County of San Francisco, State of California.
