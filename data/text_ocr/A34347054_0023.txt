BRITISH HONDURA S.S. I, Lucilo Prospero Ayuso of 54 Queen Street, Belize City do solemnly and sincerely Declare that George Forman was the father of Winifred Adelaide Forman who has been known from birth as such. The name mentioned as Gabourel and Gabrol are one and the same person. And I make this solemn declaration, conscientiously believing the same to be true; and by virtue of the provisions of The Oaths Ordinance, Chapter 20 of the Laws of British Honduras, Revised Edition, 1958. Calling Declared and subscribed at Belize City this CP: 4th day of September before me, John 1973 Justice of the Peace
