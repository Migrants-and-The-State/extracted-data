Gey-Gay 3 127/24 In re: a Yon Shee, Wife of native, 10-1 SS President Cleveland, March 20th, 1924. State of California a City and County of ) SS Photograph of San Francisco ) Gee Gay. Gee Gay, being first duly sworn, upon oath, according to law, deposes and says:- That affiant is acquainted with Yon Shee, the applicant in the above entitled case. That the said Yon Shee is the lawful wife of Gee Park. That the said Gee Park was born in the United States and is a citizen thereof, and that it has been so found and determined by competent authority. hee gay Subscribed and sworn to before me this 20% of Marchn, 1924. Motary Public.
