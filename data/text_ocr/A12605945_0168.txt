U.S. DEPARTMENT OF LABOR IMMIGRATION SERVICE OFFICE OF THE COMMISSIONER SAN FRANCISCO, CAL. 14438/6-20 November 15, 1915. United States Attorney. Postoffice Bldg., San Francisco, Cal. Sir: It will be appreciated if you will have temporarily withdrawn from the District Court's record the Immigration record in the case of Wong Quen Luck, the Chinese who was ordered discharged on habeas corpus by Judge Dooling, on the 12th instant, as this office desires to return the name to the Department of Labor as goop as possible with avview to ascertaining whether the Department desires to have an appeal taken. This office presumes, of course, that the necessary steps have been taken by you to preserve the right of appeal in the event that it is decided that one shall be taken. Respectfully, CDM/LX Exact copy as signed by EDWARD WHITE Malled this day by Commissioner. K
