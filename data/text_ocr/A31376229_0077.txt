CANADIAN EMBASSY AMBASSADE DU CANADA Washington, D.C. January 30, 1970 TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN This is to certify that Mrs. Jezrene TALBOT served to our full satisfaction as cook at the Canadian Embassy Residence from January 2, 1968 to our departure from Washington on January 30, 1970. She cooked well and imaginatively and was a congenial member of our household. She did her work conscientiously and showed herself to be a good and honest employee. CP Rth A. E. Ritchie Ambassador
