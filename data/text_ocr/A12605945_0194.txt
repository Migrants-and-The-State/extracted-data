U.S. DEPARTMENT OF LABOR National IMMIGRATION SERVICE Acchines OFFICE OF THE COMMISSIONER SAN FRANCISCO, CAL. City NO.14438/6-80 In the matter of WONG QUEN LOCK, SS "Korea" , June 21,1915. Application to land as son of merchant. Finding. After a full hearing and careful consideration of all the evidence submitted and adduced, in the matter of the application for admission to the United States of the Chinese person Wong Quan Lock, as the minor son of a. domiciled Chinese merchant, the existence of the relationship claimed to his alleged father is not established to my satisfaction. The application to land is accordingly denied, and the applicant advised of his right to appeal. Dated this 5 day of August, 1915. LL/LM (sgd.) Samuel W. Backus Commissioner. 29
