Affidavit of Support ( For use in Connection with the Issuance of Immigration Visas) I Sebastian C. Moreno, residing at 208 No. Lorena St. in the City of Los Angeles, County of Los Angeles, State of California, being duly sworn, depose and say: That I am a citizen of the Republic of Mexico, with legal residence in the United States as of July 6, 1956, under permit # A-10-548-508. That I am over twenty-one years of age and am married and have one daughter, who are at present both in Mexico. That I am steadily employed by Monarch Record Mfg. Co. of Los Angeles, Calif. at a weekly salary of $85.00. That I own no real estate. That I have never sign an affidavit of support in behalf of another person or persons. That it is my desire and intention to have, Consuelo M. Moreno, my wife and Irma Argela Moreno, my daughter, both now residing in Chihuahua, Chihuahua, Mexico, come to the United States as permanent residents. That my wife is about forty-five years of age and my daughter is about fourteen years old. That I am willing and able to receive, maintain and support the prospective immigrants mentioned above. That I am ready, willing and able to deposit, if necessary, with the United States Immigration Authorities to guarantee that such prospective immigrants will not become a public charge during their stay in the United States. That this affidavit is made by me for the sole purpose of assist- ing the American Consul and the Immigration and Naturalization Service in determining the fitness of the above mentioned applicants. Subscribed and sworn to before me this 7th day of October, 1957. Julyes Notary Public in and for the County of Los Angeles, State of California. My Commission Expires Nov. 23, 1960
