Paterson, 8/17/64 To whom it may concerns, for the best of the Nations I inform you the a Giovanni Cuone residing by Pasquale Russo 381 24 Street came in America with the excuse to see the Worlds Fier but instead he is working with Lazzara Tasty Crust Bread on Getty Ave and Madison Ave in Paterson. Please put a stop to it. Thank you, Luigi Pisapia I's for no unfid
