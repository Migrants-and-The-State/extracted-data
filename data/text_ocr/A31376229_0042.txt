23rd October, 1962. The Embassy of Nigeria presents its compliments to the Department of State and has the honor to inform the Department that Miss JEZRE ADELPHINE TALBOT, a Jamaican has been employed as a cook at the Embassy of Nigeria, Form DS-394 in respect of Miss Talbot is attached herewith in duplicate for the Department's information. Also attached is Miss Talbot's Passport No.225645 for a change in visa status please. OF Department of State, PASSINGTON D WASHINGTON D.C.
