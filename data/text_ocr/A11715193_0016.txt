UNITED STATES DEPARTMENT OF JUSTICE IMMIGRATION AND NATURALIZATION SERVICE Box 1724, Helena, Montana PLEASE REFER TO THIS FILE NUMBER Sept. 8, 1958 All 715 193 Mrs. Ida Boysun Wolf Point, Montana Dear Mrs. Boysun: with reference to your application to file a petition for naturalization, it will be necessary that you submit to this office documentary proof that you were in the United States before June 29, 1906. Such documents may be family Bible entries, deeds of record, insurance policies, or it may be affidavits by persons who knew you and your family and knew that you were residing in the United States before 1906. Old passports or other papers pertaining to your arrival are good documentary evidence. Affidavits must be notarized. Very truly yours awardwelt JAMES M. SWEET District Director
