THE SiPk S.C.EVANS, PRESIDENT. P.T.EVANS, SECRETARY. LAND F IRRIGATING CO. Riverside Cal January 11, 1916. Edward White, Commissioner, U.S. Dept. of Labor, Immigration Service, Angel Island Station, via Ferry P.O. San Francisco, California. Dear Sir:- Referring to my letter to you of December 28, 1915 regarding the certificate of WONG SHOON JUNG will again ask that you return it to me for delivery to said Chinaman. His son is here in Riverside as he was discharged and I have the papers of discharge in the habeas corpus proceedings in my office. Kindly give this matter your early attention, and oblige. Yours truly, P.T.E. Evans our of twent of
