SHERIFF'S OFFICE HENDRY COUNTY LABELLE, FLORIDA BILL MADDOX SHERIFF November 14, 1958 TO HOM IT MAY CONCERN: Concerning Yoshisuke Hatanaka's criminal record in Hendry County, Florida. Permit me to say, Yoshisuke Hatanaka, has no police record in Hendry County, Florida. Yours truly, Bill Maddox Bill Maddox, Sheriff, Hendry County, LaBelle, Florida.
