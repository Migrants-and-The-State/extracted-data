U.S. DEPARTMENT OF LABOR IMMIGRATION SERVICE OFFICE OF THE COMMISSIONER SAN FRANCISCO, CAL. 14438/6-20 December 23,1915. United States Attorney, Postoffice Bldg., San Francisco, Cal. Sir: This office is in receipt of a telegram dated December 22, 1915, from the Bureau of Immigration at Washington, which reads as follows: "Bureau is desirious U.S. Attorney prosecute appeal case Wong Quen Luck. Exhibits being returned." You will remember that the Chinese referred to was recently discharged on habeas corpus by Judge Dooling. When the exhibits referred to in the telegrap are received, they will be delivered to you in order that they may be returned to the court's record. Respectfully, Commissioner. CDM/LM
