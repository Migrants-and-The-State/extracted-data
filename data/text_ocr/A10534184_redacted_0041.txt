Magistrate Address of Magistrate The undersigned complainant being duly sworn states: COUNT ONE That beginning at a date unknown and continuing up to and including the 27th day of July, 1981 in the Southern District of California, DEFENDANT (s )Arturo GONZALEZ Hernandez, Vicente DE LA HOYA Sanchez, Leopoldo ALVARADO Gonzalez and others unlawfully, willfully, and knowingly did combine, conspire, and agree together and with each other, and with divers other persons unknown to the complainant to violate certain laws of the United States, to wit, to violate Title 8, United States Code, Section 1324. It was a part of the Said Conspiracy that the defendant and others would willfully and knowingly encourage and induce, and attempt to encourage and induce, directly and indirectly, the entry into the United States of aliens not lawfully en- titled to enter or reside within the United States, in violation of Title 18, United States Code, Section 371.
