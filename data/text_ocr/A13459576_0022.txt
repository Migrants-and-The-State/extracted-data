In re Lee Gim Ngow, Minor son of Lee Yip Sang, a Native Born Citizen of the United States. State of California, City and County of SS. San Francisco. Lee Yip Sang, being first duly sworn, deposes and says: That affiant was born in the United States and is a citizen thereof, and that it has been so found and determined by competent authority. That affiant makes this affidavit in order to identify Lee Gim Ngow as his son. That the said Lee Gim Ngow is a lawful son of affiant and is a minor. That the said Lee Gim Ngow is a citizen of the United States, under and by virtue of the laws thereof, affiant, his father, being a native born citizen thereof. That said Lee Gim Ngow is now in China, but is about to come to the United States to take up his residence therein with affiant. That a photograph of affiant and a photograph of said Lee Gim Ngow are hereunto annexed. Subscribed and sworn to before me this 28 day of October, 1913.
