- BRITISH EMBASSY WASHINGTON October 11, 1961. Dear Sir, The bearer of this letter, Miss Jezrene Talbot, is employed by my wife on domestic work. I should be much obliged if you would kindly grant her the necessary extension of her temporary stay in this country to enable her to continue in our employment. I myself am employed by the British Embassy in Washington. Yours faithfully, Ruderwear R. W. Roseveare District Director, Immigration and Naturalization Service, (U. S. Department of Justice), Room 306, Old Post Office Building, 12th and Pennsylvania Ave. N.W., Washington 25, D.C.
