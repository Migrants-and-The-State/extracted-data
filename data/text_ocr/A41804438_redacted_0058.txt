A NON REACTIVE result implies that no Anti HIV-I or Anti HIV-II antibodies have been detected in the sample by this method. This means that either the patient has not been exposed to HIV-I or HIV-II infection or the sample has been tested during the "WINDOW PHASE" ( before the development of detectable levels of antibodies). 2. A PROVISIONALLY REACTIVE / BORDERL REACTIVE result suggests the possibility of HIV-I or/and HIV-II infection.
