1958 OCT RECEI 27 AM 16:12 AFFIDAVIT We the undersigned being duly sworn hereby state that we were well acquainted with the parents of Ida Boysun who were Mr & Mrs Nikola Klym who resided in Billings County North Dakota. We state further that the Klym family came to Billings County in the month of May 1900, in witness wereof we have set our hands and seals this 11th Day of October 1958 John N Ewoniuk Belfield ND Age 76 metro Metro Grosulak Belfield N D Age 77 Subscribed and sworn to before me this 11th Day of October 1958. CARL INDERGAARD, Notary Public, STARK CO., N. DAK. My commission expires MARCH 27, 1959,
