BERNARD PEARSON, M. D. 1235 WESTWOOD BLVD. WESTWOOD VILLAGE LOS ANGELES 24 August 18,1944. To Whom it May Concern: Re: Mrs Dora Paps Because of extreme nervousness and weakened physical condition, it is my recommendation that Mrs. Paps be not required to appear in Court Monday, August 21, 1944. Yours very truly, Benard Reason Bernard Pearson, M.D.
