35047/327 March 8, 1935. Inspector in Charge, Immigration & Naturalization Service, Los Angeles, Calif. In reply to your request for verification of landing of PHILIP MICHAEL PAPS, your file 8097/4240, you are informed that while the crew list of the SS "DRYDEN" covering her arrival at this port on November 5, 1928 is not on file in this office, investigation of the records of the Shipping Commissioner show that this vessel ar- rived at this port coastwise from Honolulu on that date. His records also show that PHILIP M. PAPS was signed on as a member of the crew of that vessel in Shanghai, China, on August 20, 1928, and paid off in New Orleans, La., November 5, 1928. EUGENZ KESSLER District Director, GBS/D New Orleans District. STON MAR 12 1935 LOS Exhibit La File No 16542/2238 ANGELES LOCAL Case 05
